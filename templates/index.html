Layer Reference Guide

True Color (MODIS)
Natural-color composite. Cloud shapes, land, ocean. Daily, ~3-5h latency.

IR Thermal (GOES-East)
Infrared brightness temp. Cold tops = deep convection = heavy rain. Every 10 min.
🌧️
GPM Precipitation
Direct rain rate from GPM satellite. Near-real-time, 30-min updates.
💧
Water Vapor
Mid-level moisture. Atmospheric river tracking and dry air intrusions.
